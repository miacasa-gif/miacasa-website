Our Story

MiaCasa is a women-owned small business built with a simple idea — to create a place where guests feel genuinely welcome.

← Back to MiaCasa Homestays

What started as a personal project has grown into a small collection of homes in Hanoi, each designed with care, warmth, and attention to detail.

We focus on what matters most: clean, comfortable spaces, honest and transparent communication, and a stay that feels easy and relaxed.

Every guest matters to us. We are not a large hotel chain — and that is intentional.

We believe in thoughtful hospitality, where small details make a big difference.

We look forward to hosting you in Hanoi.
