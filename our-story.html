Building MiaCasa Hanoi

MiaCasa Hanoi became a calm, quiet space — tucked away from the noise, but still close to everything that makes Hanoi special. It's designed for travelers who want a slower, more grounded stay. A place to come back to after a long day in the city.

Creating MiaCasa Old Quarter

After MiaCasa Hanoi, the idea was to create something with a different energy. That became MiaCasa Old Quarter.

Here, the focus is not quiet — it's connection. A full apartment in the heart of the Old Quarter, surrounded by street food, night walks, and the constant rhythm of the city. It's designed for groups and families — people who want to experience Hanoi together.

Same care. Different feeling.

Meet the Hosts

MiaCasa is built and run by Linh and Ngọc — long-time friends who became business partners through a shared vision.

Linh

Professional interior designer who focuses on creating spaces that feel calm, warm, and quietly beautiful — the kind of places she would want to stay in herself.

Ngọc

Works in hospitality and brings a deep understanding of what guests actually need — from comfort to small thoughtful touches that make a stay memorable.

Together, they built MiaCasa from scratch — learning, adjusting, and improving along the way. Linh shapes how it looks. Ngọc shapes how it feels.

What Stays the Same

Even though the spaces are different, the intention behind them is the same.

  • Thoughtful spaces instead of over-designed ones
  • Honest communication instead of scripted service
  • Real hospitality instead of transactions
