About the Stay

In the vibrant
Old Quarter

Located in the vibrant Old Quarter, MiaCasa Old Quarter places you right in the center of Hanoi's culture, food, and nightlife.

The apartment features three queen beds, making it ideal for families or small groups who want to stay together in one comfortable space.

Step outside and you are instantly surrounded by local cafés, street food, and historic streets.

⚠ Heads up: The neighbourhood is lively and can be noisy at night. Access is via steep stairs — not ideal for young children, elderly guests, or anyone with mobility concerns.

House Rules

A few things to keep in mind

To ensure everyone has a wonderful stay, we ask all guests to respect these simple guidelines.

  • Check-in from 2:00 PM (self check-in)
  • MiaCasaHanoi: check-out by 12:00 PM (noon)
  • MiaCasaOldQuarter: check-out by 11:00 AM

🤫
Noise & Guests
  • Quiet hours 10:00 PM – 7:00 AM
  • No unregistered overnight guests
  • Be mindful of neighbours
  • Groups of 4+ need prior notice
🐾
Smoking & Pets
  • No smoking indoors
  • Balcony/terrace smoking only
  • Pets welcome — additional charge applies, please inform the host in advance
  • Candles permitted with care
🏠
Property Care
  • Treat the space as your own home
  • Report damages promptly
  • No shoes inside — slippers provided
  • Use designated rubbish bins

Legal Requirement
  • Vietnamese law requires all guests to provide a copy of their passport or national ID

  • Bookings cannot be confirmed without this document
  • Information is used solely for local authority registration

Payments & Cancellation

Heart of
Hoan Kiem & Old Quarter

📍 38 P. Lương Ngọc Quyến, Hàng Buồm, Hoàn Kiếm, Hanoi

  • ~5 min walk to Hoàn Kiếm Lake
  • ~2 min walk to Lương Văn Can Street (Old Quarter's busiest)
  • ~10 min walk to Đồng Xuân Market
  • ~15 min by Grab to Hanoi Railway Station

Staying in Hoàn Kiếm means walking distance to Hoàn Kiếm Lake, easy access to night markets and street food, and a fully immersive Old Quarter experience. Hàng Buồm Street — one of Hanoi's oldest trading streets — is right outside your door. If you prefer a quieter, residential base away from the tourist crowds, our MiaCasa Hanoi rooms near Train Street are a great alternative.
