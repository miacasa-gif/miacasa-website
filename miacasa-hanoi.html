About the Stay

A quiet, local
stay in Hanoi

MiaCasaHanoi is designed for travelers who want a quieter, more local experience while staying close to Hanoi's main attractions.

Located near Hanoi Railway Station and Văn Miếu – Quốc Tử Giám, the property offers easy access to the Old Quarter without the constant noise and crowds. If your priority is being steps from Hoàn Kiếm Lake and the Old Quarter's night markets, explore our MiaCasa Old Quarter apartment instead.

Each room is thoughtfully designed with natural light, wooden accents, and a warm, minimalist aesthetic.

Location

Near Train Street
& Old Quarter

📍 92 Ngh. 51 Ng. Linh Quang, Văn Chương, Hanoi

  • ~5 min walk to Hanoi Train Street (Railway Street)
  • ~8 min walk to Văn Miếu – Quốc Tử Giám (Temple of Literature)
  • ~10 min by Grab to the Old Quarter and Hoàn Kiếm Lake
  • ~3 min walk to Hanoi Railway Station (trains to Sapa, Hội An)

Staying near Hanoi Railway Station means you are 5–10 minutes from the Old Quarter, close to Văn Miếu (Temple of Literature), and well connected for trains and day trips. The neighbourhood of Văn Chương is quiet and residential — a welcome contrast to the buzzing streets of the Old Quarter. If you prefer to be right in the centre of Hàng Buồm and Hoàn Kiếm, our MiaCasa Old Quarter apartment may suit you better.

House Rules

A few things to keep in mind

To ensure everyone has a wonderful stay, we ask all guests to respect these simple guidelines.

🕐
Check-in / Check-out
  • Check-in from 2:00 PM (self check-in)
  • MiaCasaHanoi: check-out by 12:00 PM (noon)
  • MiaCasaOldQuarter: check-out by 11:00 AM

  • Luggage storage available at MiaCasaHanoi — longer durations may incur an extra charge
🤫
Noise & Guests
  • Quiet hours 10:00 PM – 7:00 AM
  • No unregistered overnight guests
  • Be mindful of neighbours
  • Groups of 4+ need prior notice
🐾
Smoking & Pets
  • No smoking indoors
  • Balcony/terrace smoking only
  • Pets welcome — additional charge applies, please inform the host in advance
  • Candles permitted with care

Property Care
  • Treat the space as your own home
  • Report damages promptly
  • No shoes inside — slippers provided
  • Use designated rubbish bins

  • Vietnamese law requires all guests to provide a copy of their passport or national ID

  • Free cancellation up to 48h prior
